Robotic Series: Deathbot (2024)

Crafted from a marriage of woods, metals, and plastics, this unsettlingly rigid parody of a human limb twitches against its restraints, as programmed. Bound by industrial grade duct tape, it swings a sharp kitchen blade, rhythmically cutting into its binding, a counterpoint to the desperate futility of the action. This kinetic scupture, informally called "Cut Loose", explores the potential anxieties of advancing technology. Trapped in a mechanical struggle by its programming, it asks the observer, could technology one day turn its ingenuity against the very restraints we place upon them?
