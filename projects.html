Ice Spice Racing (Videogame)

A silly video game I made in 2022 for fun. The premise was supposed to be something like Mario Kart but themed after American rapper Ice Spice. The majority of the game was made in the Godot game engine, and the 3D models I made in Blender. It was simple but worked, and I had genuine fun making it. Fun fact: I produced the title screen remix in Ableton.
